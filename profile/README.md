## About War Thunder

War Thunder is a military combat game that features large-scale battles across air, land, and naval forces. Players control a wide variety of vehicles, including aircraft, tanks, and ships, and engage in multiplayer combat scenarios based on historical and modern warfare.

The game focuses on realistic physics, vehicle handling, and combat mechanics. Players can customize their vehicles, upgrade equipment, and choose different combat roles depending on their preferred playstyle.

With multiple game modes, progression systems, and continuous updates, War Thunder delivers an immersive experience built around tactical gameplay, teamwork, and large-scale battles.
